How to Make a Cup of Tea

Boil Water
Fill a kettle with fresh water and bring it to a boil.

Prepare the Teapot or Cup
Place a tea bag or loose tea leaves into your teapot or cup.

Pour Hot Water
Carefully pour the boiling water over the tea bag or leaves.

Steep the Tea
Let the tea steep for 3 to 5 minutes, depending on how strong you like it.

Remove the Tea Bag or Leaves
Take out the tea bag or strain the leaves from the teapot.

Add Extras (Optional)
Add sugar, honey, milk, or lemon if you like.

Enjoy Your Tea
Stir, sip, and enjoy your freshly brewed cup of tea!
